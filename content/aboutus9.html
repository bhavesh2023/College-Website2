Department of Computer Science and Engineering

Previous Next

Department of Computer Science & Engineering came into existence in 1994. The first degree program offered by the Department was B.E. (Computer Engineering) in affiliation with University of Rajasthan. The first batch graduated in 1996. Another degree programme BE (Information Technology) was initiated in 2001. Since 2004 after MREC was granted status of deemed university and included as NIT (National Institute of Technology), all BE programmes came to be known as B. Tech. In January 2004, first admissions to PhD programme took place. In 2008, M. Tech. (Computer Engineering) programme was initiated. At present, Computer Engineering Department is offering following degree programmes:

  • B.Tech (Computer Engineering)
  • M.Tech (Computer Engineering)
  • Ph. D
    • Faculty at Computer Engineering believes in open interaction with students who are encouraged to participate in academic, research and extra-curricular activities. Students have never misplaced trust put in them by department and have done well in academic and industry alike.

    Department Vision & Mission

    Vision Statement

    To be a Centre of Excellence in Computer Science and Engineering domain and work with industry on cutting edge technology to address current and emerging challenges in global perspective for the development of society.

    Mission Statement

    To impart quality engineering education, enhance problem solving skills, foster research and innovation, encourage entrepreneurship and mold students of integrity and ethics to provide leadership with social sensitivity for the betterment of the country and humanity as a whole.
